About Chakradev Ayurveda

Founded on the belief that ancient wisdom and modern medicine are not opposites — they are partners in healing.

🌿

Our Mission — Making Authentic Ayurveda Accessible

Chakradev Ayurveda was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to make authentic, classical Ayurvedic healthcare accessible to every person, regardless of where they live.

Named after the divine healer Chakradeva — author of the Chakradatta, one of Ayurveda's foundational medical texts — our platform honours the depth and precision of classical Ayurvedic medicine while bringing it into the digital age.

We believe that genuine healing requires understanding the root cause of imbalance, not merely suppressing symptoms. Every consultation, every product, and every article on this platform reflects that philosophy.

Our Journey

2018

Founded in Kerala

Chakradev Ayurveda began as a small clinic in Kerala, offering in-person consultations and herbal preparations rooted in classical Ayurveda.

2019

First 100 Patients

Within our first year, we had served over 100 patients — many referred by word of mouth after experiencing significant improvements in chronic conditions.

2020

Moving Online

Responding to the need for accessible healthcare, we launched our online consultation platform — bringing Ayurvedic care to patients across India and beyond.

2021

E-Shop Launched

We launched our curated online store, offering lab-tested, doctor-recommended Ayurvedic products sourced from certified organic farms.

2022

Knowledge Hub

Our blog and Dosha Analysis tool launched, giving thousands of users free access to authentic Ayurvedic education and personalised body-type assessments.

2024

1,500+ Patients Served

Today, Chakradev Ayurveda has helped over 1,500 patients across India and internationally achieve lasting health through authentic Ayurvedic care.
