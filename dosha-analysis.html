The Three Doshas

Every person has a unique blend of all three Doshas. Your dominant Dosha shapes your physical traits, emotions, digestion, and personality.

Vata — Air & Space Creative, quick, light, dry, cold, mobile
Pitta — Fire & Water Sharp, intense, warm, oily, focused
Kapha — Earth & Water Stable, heavy, cool, smooth, compassionate

How to Take the Test

For each of the 32 observations, choose the option that most accurately describes your natural tendencies — not how you feel today. Think of your whole life.
